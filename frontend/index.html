How It Works

Simple. Fast. Life-Saving.

Three steps to connect donors with those in need — anywhere across Bangladesh, anytime.

Register & Profile

Create your free account in under 2 minutes. Set your blood type, district, and availability — your profile goes live immediately in our national network.

Find a Match

Our smart matching system instantly connects recipients with compatible donors in their area. Search by blood type, location, or urgency level.

Donate & Save

Coordinate directly with the recipient or their family. Show up, donate, and become someone's hero. Every drop truly counts.

Blood Types

Know Your Type

Understanding blood compatibility is crucial. Find donors or recipients by their blood group across Bangladesh.

A+
High Demand
Can donate to A+ and AB+. Compatible with A- and O- donors.
A−
Rare
Can donate to all A and AB types. Universal donor for A-types.
B+
Common
Can donate to B+ and AB+. Compatible with B- and O- donors.
B−
Rare
Can donate to all B and AB types. Critical type for hospitals.
O+
Most Common
Most needed blood type. Can donate to all positive blood types.
O−
Universal Donor
The universal donor. Accepted by all blood types — critical in emergencies.
AB+
Universal Receiver
Can receive from all blood types. Plasma donors for all blood types.
AB−
Very Rare
Rarest blood type. Universal plasma donor — extremely valuable.
