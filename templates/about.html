The Project

What Is VitalDrop?

A mission-driven platform designed to bridge the critical gap between blood donors and patients in Bangladesh.

Real-Time Emergency Feed

Live blood requests from hospitals across Bangladesh, filtered by blood group and urgency — so donors can respond within minutes of a request being posted.

Donor Management

Registered donors can manage their profiles, track donation history, and receive alerts for compatible blood group emergencies near their location.

Secure Admin Dashboard

Hospital coordinators and admins can post, manage, and close blood requests through a dedicated dashboard with role-based access control.

Donation Education Hub

A comprehensive guide covering eligibility, blood type compatibility, preparation tips, and aftercare — making donation approachable for first-timers.
